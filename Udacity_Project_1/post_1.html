Which of these languages are used/growing in the business/tech world

Admittedly this section, along with the final section, is very opinion based. There are new businesses and tech companies forming everyday so for this one I will just say "DO..YOUR..RESEARCH!". Just pick whichever language you may be interested in and research what companies and new businesses are out there. Are speakers of said language in demand in a certain field? For example, over the past year along my journey of getting into the tech field I have met literally hundreds of Hindi and Punjabi speakers. Thus I found it to be worthwhile to begin learning Hindi. I still haven't reached far in the language but I have already had beautiful experiences with fellow "techies" when I simply greet them in Hindi. People seem to want to connect and help you even more when you make such efforts to communicate in their language. On the reverse side, what if you cant find speakers of your target language as easily in the business/tech space? Does this mean it's not worth it to learn? ....NO! A language that isn't currently viewed as a language of tech is what brought me into tech!... Sometimes if you can't easily find the things you desire you have to put yourself in the position to create it!
