Thus my tech journey began...but let me tell you some other things about me. Firstly let me explain that because of my connection to the Swahili language, in many business situations (translation/teaching Swahili) I go my the name Mfalme Jabali Ekevu (mmmm-fall-may jah-bah-lee eh-cav-oo). So please do not be alarmed by that name on Social Media, I have grown to love the name and often use it outside of business. In other news, my parents were born in the island of Jamaica, my father in Trelawny and my mother in St. Andrew. I however was born in Hempstead, Long Island, NY in July of 1993.
